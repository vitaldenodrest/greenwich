\paragraph{Exact solution.}
We consider the sourceless Helmholtz equation in homogeneous media
$
\Delta \phi + k^2 \phi = 0
$
in 
$
\mathbb{R}
$.
The solutions are defined by
$
\phi(x) = c e^{ikx}
$
with any constant $c$.
